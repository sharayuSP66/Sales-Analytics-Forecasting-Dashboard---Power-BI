# Sales-Analytics-Forecasting-Dashboard---Power-BI
Interactive Power BI dashboard for sales analytics, business insights, and sales forecasting using retail sales data.


* ###### **Project Overview :**

This project focuses on analyzing Super Store sales data using Power BI to uncover business insights and build an interactive dashboard for decision-making. The dashboard enables users to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), analyze sales trends, identify top-performing regions and products, and forecast future sales based on historical order data.





* ###### **Business Problem :**

Retail businesses generate large amounts of sales data, making it difficult to quickly identify trends, high-performing products, and regional performance. This dashboard transforms raw sales data into interactive visualizations that support informed business decisions and future sales planning.





* ###### **Dataset Information :**

The project uses a Super Store Sales dataset containing **5,901 transaction records** and **23 attributes**, including order details, customer information, product categories, sales, profit, shipping modes, payment methods, and regional data. The dataset was analyzed using Power BI to generate business insights and forecast future sales trends.



* ###### **Objectives :**


&#x20;    1. Analyze sales performance across different regions and customer segments.

&#x20;    2. Identify trends in sales and profit over time.

&#x20;    3. Monitor product category and sub-category performance.

&#x20;    4. Analyze customer payment preferences and shipping modes.

&#x20;    5. Forecast future sales using historical order data.

&#x20;    6. Provide interactive dashboards to support business decision-making.

&#x20;      1. Consumer segment generated the highest sales revenue.

&#x20;      2. California contributed the highest overall sales.

&#x20;      3. Standard shipping mode was used for the majority of orders.

&#x20;      5. Office Supplies emerged as one of the highest-selling product categories.

&#x20;      6. Forecasting indicates a steady upward sales trend, helping support inventory and business planning.
